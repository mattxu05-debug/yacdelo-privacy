Privacy Policy

Yacdelo CAM - Camera Control Application

1. Information We Collect

Yacdelo CAM collects minimal information necessary to provide camera control functionality:

  • Account Information: Email address and encrypted password when you create an account. You may also use the app as a guest without an account.
  • Device Information: Camera SSID, IP address, and firmware version for device management purposes. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Feedback Data: When you submit feedback, we collect your email, issue description, and optional image attachments.

2. Location Permission

This app requests location permission solely to detect the camera's WiFi network name (SSID). This is an Android system requirement for WiFi SSID detection on Android 9 and above. We do not collect, store, or transmit any location data.

3. Camera Data

All camera communications (video streaming, photo capture, file downloads) occur directly between your phone and the camera over a local WiFi connection. No camera data passes through our servers.

4. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Camera settings, saved device list, and downloaded media files are stored only on your device.
  • Server Storage: Only account credentials (email and encrypted password) are stored on our server for authentication purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

6. Data Security

Account passwords are encrypted before transmission and storage. Camera communications use local WiFi connections that do not traverse the internet.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Use the app as a guest without creating an account
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Revoke location permission at any time in your device settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@yacdelo.com
